About Us

Altagether is a group of organizers and Altadena residents working to help neighbors connect with each other to navigate all steps of the post-wildfire process, keep residents in Altadena, and work together for collective recovery.

We use the Neighborhood Captain model to help connect our neighbors to each other.

The Neighborhood Captain Model

Neighborhood Captains play a vital role in ensuring no one is left behind in our community's recovery. If you're connected to your neighbors, organized, and care deeply about our community, you can step up as a Neighborhood Captain. In this role, you'll help keep your community informed, supported, and empowered every step of the way.
