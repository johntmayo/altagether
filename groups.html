Working Groups

Working Groups are small, focused teams within Altagether made up of Neighborhood Captains who want to go deeper on a particular topic, challenge, or opportunity tied to our recovery and organizing work in Altadena. They’re meant to be containers for learning, collaboration, and collective action.

These groups are all volunteer run by people navigating their own recovery, so please be patient and understanding when reaching out.

These groups are open to take questions from the public and to new members, including folks who are not Neighborhood Captains with Altagether.
