Community Forum on SCE Undergrounding

Recorded October 22, 2025, this virtual community forum brings together representatives from Southern California Edison and Los Angeles County to discuss plans to underground power lines in neighborhoods affected by the Eaton Fire. The event includes an overview of plans directly impacting Altadena and a resident-moderated Q&A featuring questions from the community. Co-hosted by ACONA and Altagether.
